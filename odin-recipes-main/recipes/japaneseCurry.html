Japanese Curry

Photo of Japanese Curry with Fried Pork

All it requires is potatoes, carrots, onions, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Meat
  • Curry base

Steps

  1. Cut all ingredients into large one inch chunks
  2. Brown onions, put in all the ingredients, boil and cook, done.
